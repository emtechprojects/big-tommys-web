Made Fresh for all occassions.


We take pride in our products and believe our customers deserve the best, so our cakes are made without preservatives and fresh daily to ensure optimal freshness! Rain or shine we deliver to doors across Sydney metro.

Premium Ingredients.


Our desserts are handmade in Sydney with a strong focus on using premium ingredients. We highlight the natural sweetness from fresh fruit produced by trusted local farmers to bring balance to the decadence.
